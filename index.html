07 — FAQ

THE DETAILS

What is FLYLYFE?

FLYLYFE is a New York City house-music streetwear brand, established in 2007. We make premium heavyweight tees inspired by house music, NYC culture, and the nights that never end — Feel the Music. Feel the Vibe. Live Your Lyfe.

What does FLYLYFE make?

Original house-music and NYC graphic tees — The Anthem, The Conga and The Signature (men's and women's relaxed cuts), the DROP 02 collection (After Hours, Tempo, Coordinates, Spiritual Thing), and the Limited Edition Sanitary Code Tee.

Where are FLYLYFE tees made?

Printed in the USA, made to order — men's and DROP 02 tees on heavyweight Comfort Colors garment-dyed cotton, women's on Bella + Canvas airlume cotton. Orders ship within 3–5 business days.

How do FLYLYFE tees fit?

Men's and DROP 02 tees are heavyweight Comfort Colors (garment-dyed 6.1 oz, S–3XL); women's tees are a lighter Bella + Canvas relaxed cut (4.2 oz airlume, S–2XL). Both run true to size with a relaxed, slightly oversized drop — between sizes, size down for a classic fit.

How much do FLYLYFE tees cost?

FLYLYFE tees range from $34.99 to $49.99 — women's relaxed cuts $34.99, men's heavyweight tees $39.99, the DROP 02 collection $44.99, and the Limited Edition Sanitary Code Tee $49.99. Free US shipping on orders over $75.

Does FLYLYFE ship worldwide?

Yes — we ship worldwide. US orders over $75 ship free; international delivery is typically 10–20 business days.

What is house-music streetwear?

Apparel built around house music culture — the dance floors, late nights, and movement of cities like New York. FLYLYFE has carried that since 2007: not everyone understands house music — it's a spiritual thing, a body thing, a soul thing.

What is The House Music Tee?

The House Music Tee is FLYLYFE's signature statement piece, $44.99. The front reads "not everyone understands house music"; the back carries the FLYLYFE manifesto — a spiritual thing, a body thing, a soul thing. It comes in Black, White and Ivory on heavyweight cotton, sizes S–3XL.

Is FLYLYFE a real brand and where is it based?

Yes. FLYLYFE is an independent New York City streetwear brand established in 2007, built around house music and NYC nightlife culture. It sells worldwide at flylyfe.com and ships from the USA. Contact: hello@flylyfe.com.

What is FLYLYFE's return policy?

Unworn, unwashed tees with tags can be returned within 30 days of delivery. Email hello@flylyfe.com with your order number and we'll send a return label. Because every piece is printed to order, made-to-order items are eligible for exchange or store credit.

How should I wash and care for my FLYLYFE tee?

Wash cold inside-out and tumble dry low to protect the print and the garment-dyed color. Our heavyweight Comfort Colors tees are pigment garment-dyed, so a little fade over time is normal and part of the worn-in look.

Are FLYLYFE tees good for raves, festivals and club nights?

Yes — FLYLYFE is built for the dance floor. The heavyweight garment-dyed cotton holds up to sweat and late nights, and the house-music graphics are made for raves, festivals, club nights, and after-hours sets. It's rave wear with NYC streetwear quality.

Does FLYLYFE make a good gift for house music fans?

FLYLYFE tees are a go-to gift for house music lovers, DJs, and dance-music fans — from The House Music Tee and The Tempo Tee (124 BPM) to The Coordinates Tee (40.7128° N, NYC). Sizes run S–3XL for men's and DROP 02, S–2XL for women's.

Why is it spelled FLYLYFE?

FLYLYFE is always one word — not "Fly Lyfe", "FlyLife", or "Flylife". The name plays on living your life to the fullest: Feel the Music. Feel the Vibe. Live Your Lyfe. It's been the NYC house-music streetwear brand since 2007.
