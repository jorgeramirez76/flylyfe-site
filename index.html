07 — FAQ

THE DETAILS

What is FLYLYFE?

FLYLYFE is a New York City house-music streetwear brand, established in 2007. We make premium heavyweight tees inspired by house music, NYC culture, and the nights that never end — Feel the Music. Feel the Vibe. Live Your Lyfe.

What does FLYLYFE make?

Original house-music and NYC graphic tees — The Anthem, The Conga and The Signature (men's and women's relaxed cuts), the DROP 02 collection (After Hours, Tempo, Coordinates, Spiritual Thing), and the Limited Edition Sanitary Code Tee.

Where are FLYLYFE tees made?

Printed in the USA, made to order — men's and DROP 02 tees on heavyweight Comfort Colors garment-dyed cotton, women's on Bella + Canvas airlume cotton. Orders ship within 3–5 business days.

How do FLYLYFE tees fit?

Men's and DROP 02 tees are heavyweight Comfort Colors (garment-dyed 6.1 oz, S–3XL); women's tees are a lighter Bella + Canvas relaxed cut (4.2 oz airlume, S–2XL). Both run true to size with a relaxed, slightly oversized drop — between sizes, size down for a classic fit.

How much do FLYLYFE tees cost?

FLYLYFE tees range from $34.99 to $49.99 — women's relaxed cuts $34.99, men's heavyweight tees $39.99, the DROP 02 collection $44.99, and the Limited Edition Sanitary Code Tee $49.99. Free US shipping on orders over $75.

Does FLYLYFE ship worldwide?

Yes — we ship worldwide. US orders over $75 ship free; international delivery is typically 10–20 business days.

What is house-music streetwear?

Apparel built around house music culture — the dance floors, late nights, and movement of cities like New York. FLYLYFE has carried that since 2007: not everyone understands house music — it's a spiritual thing, a body thing, a soul thing.
